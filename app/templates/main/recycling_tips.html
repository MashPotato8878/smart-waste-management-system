General Recycling Do's and Don'ts

Do's
  • Rinse containers before recycling.
  • Flatten cardboard boxes to save space.
  • Check local recycling rules for accepted items.
  • Keep recyclables dry and clean.
  • Remove caps and lids from bottles and jars.
Don'ts
  • Don't recycle greasy pizza boxes or dirty food containers.
  • Don't put plastic bags in the recycling bin.
  • Don't recycle batteries or electronics with regular recyclables.
  • Don't mix non-recyclables with recyclables.
  • Don't recycle items smaller than a credit card.
